Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the following apps developed by Meredith McClain ("we", "our", "us"):

Data Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not require user accounts, logins, or registration of any kind.

Data Storage

All data you enter into our apps (such as financial calculations, settings, or preferences) is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data and it never leaves your device.

Photo Access (PinkShrink)

PinkShrink requests access to your photo library solely to compress photos you select. All photo processing happens entirely on your device. Photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

Analytics & Tracking

Our apps do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, cookies, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage or behavior in any way.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Advertising

Our apps contain no advertisements and do not use any advertising frameworks or networks.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our apps are safe for use by people of all ages.
