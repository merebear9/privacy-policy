Privacy Policy

Meredith McClain Apps · Last updated March 2026

This privacy policy applies to the following apps developed by Meredith McClain:

🔥 MoneyBloom: FIRE Calculator — Financial independence calculator
🎖️ AFT Calculator: Army Fitness — Army Fitness Test score calculator

Data Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We have no servers, no database, and no analytics.

Local Storage Only

All data you enter — including scores, settings, soldier profiles, and body composition records — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

No Accounts

No account, login, or registration is required to use any of our apps.

No Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or any third-party services. We do not participate in any ad networks. We do not use Apple's advertising identifier.

No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. There is no data to share because we don't collect any.

Data Deletion

If you delete an app, all data associated with it is permanently removed from your device. There is nothing stored elsewhere to delete.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (for example, by adding optional analytics), we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
