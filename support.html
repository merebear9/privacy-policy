Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. If you delete the app, your data is gone.

Do I need an account?

No. None of our apps require a login, account, or registration.

Are the AFT scoring tables accurate?

Yes. Scoring data comes directly from the official army.mil AFT Scoring Scales PDF (Approved 15 May 2025, Effective 1 June 2025). Body composition uses the DoD Waist-to-Height Ratio standard effective January 1, 2026.

Can I transfer my data to a new phone?

If you use iCloud backup and restore your new phone from that backup, your app data should transfer automatically.

Is the app affiliated with the U.S. Army?

No. These apps are independently developed and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the U.S. Army or Department of Defense.
